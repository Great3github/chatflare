Chatflare, by Matt

Powered by Cloudflare, Typescript, and an HTML nerd.

Rules:
- You must use your real name, or have your real name included in your display name.
- Blank display names are not allowed

How to use this service:
To create your own chatroom, you need to put your chatroom name after the URL, like this:

http://chatflare.matts-apps.workers.dev/[Your chatroom name here.]

You are not in a chatroom.
